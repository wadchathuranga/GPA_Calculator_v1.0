This is a Grade Point Average (GPA) calculator mobile application that computes GPA
based on course credits and the achieved grade.

In this app computes semester GPA, Year GPA and final GPA.

If your university computes GPA with different weights for each year separately
this app allows you to enter the weight according to your university preference.
